How We Work

Our Process

From initial conversation to funding, we move with speed and transparency.

1

Introduction

Share your deal overview via our submission form or a direct call. We respond within 48 hours with initial feedback.

2

Preliminary Review

We review financial materials and business fundamentals. If aligned with our criteria, we issue a preliminary term sheet within 1-2 weeks.

3

Due Diligence

Thorough analysis of operations, financials, market position, and management. We work collaboratively and respect your time.

4

Closing & Funding

Final documentation and closing. We fund and become a long-term capital partner committed to your success.
